# Analysis-of-AI-Jobs-Dataset-With-Tableau-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard that explores trends in the global AI job market, focusing on salary distribution, experience levels, job titles, employment types, education levels, remote work, and country demand.
<img width="1152" height="547" alt="Analysis of AI Jobs Dataset" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6118fdff-c7e5-4031-88a0-10ac2fdd02f6" />

The goal is to uncover **actionable insights** for:
- Aspiring AI & Data professionals
- Recruiters and hiring managers
- Organizations planning AI workforce strategies

The dashboard is designed to answer **real-world career and business questions** using clean, visual storytelling.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Tableau** – Data visualization & dashboard design  
- **Microsoft Excel** – Initial data cleaning and preprocessing  
- **AI Jobs Dataset** – Industry-wide job listings and salary data  

---

##  Key Business Questions Answered
- Which AI job roles pay the highest on average?
- How does **experience level** impact salary?
- Which **countries** are hiring the most AI professionals?
- Does **remote work** influence compensation?
- How does **education level** affect salary expectations?
- Which **employment types** offer higher salary ranges?

---

##  Dashboard Insights

###  Top 5 Average Salaries by Job Title
This visualization highlights the **highest-paying AI roles**, including:
- AI Specialists  
- AI Architects  
- Machine Learning Engineers  
- Head of AI  
- AI Researchers  

 **Insight:** Leadership and highly specialized roles consistently command the highest salaries.

---

###  Salary vs Experience Level
Experience levels analyzed:
- Entry (EN)
- Mid (MI)
- Senior (SE)
- Executive (EX)

 **Insight:** Salary increases sharply with experience, with **Executive-level roles earning nearly 3x entry-level salaries**.

---

###  Top 5 Countries Hiring for AI Jobs
Countries with the highest AI job demand include:
- Germany
- Denmark
- France
- Canada
- Austria

 **Insight:** Europe dominates AI hiring, indicating strong regional investment in AI innovation.

---

###  Remote Ratio vs Salary
This heatmap examines how **remote work availability** affects salary across AI roles.

 **Insight:**  
- Roles like **AI Software Engineer** and **NLP Engineer** show strong salary performance even with high remote ratios.
- Remote work does **not necessarily reduce pay** in AI careers.

---

###  Education Level vs Salary
Education levels compared:
- Associate
- Bachelor
- Master
- PhD

 **Insight:**  
- **Master’s degree holders earn the highest average salary**.
- PhD salaries are competitive but not always the top, suggesting **industry experience matters greatly**.

---

###  Top 5 Salary Ranges by Employment Type
Employment types analyzed:
- Full-Time (FT)
- Contract (CT)
- Freelance (FL)
- Part-Time (PT)

 **Insight:**  
- **Full-time roles dominate higher salary ranges**
- Contract roles can be lucrative at senior levels

---

##  Key Takeaways
- AI leadership and architecture roles offer the highest compensation
- Experience level is the strongest salary driver
- Europe is a major hub for AI hiring
- Remote work remains financially viable in AI careers
- A Master’s degree offers the best salary advantage on average
